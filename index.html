About
the Founder

Established in 2013, SYCB is the brainchild of Golam Ahmed Khan whose father spearheaded one of the first most advanced textile mills in Bangladesh with machinery from Taiwan in the 90’s.

Over the course of time, SYCB has emerged as one of the leading apparel solutions company for start-ups in North America, France, UK, and Saudi Arabia from its headquarters in Toronto, Canada.

G A Khan’s leadership, insight, experience, and network allow for unmatched opportunities for the brands he works with. G is inspired by the great people he comes across in this journey whether its an aspiring designer, inventor, musician or artist, or affiliates of Supreme, NBA coaches, Olympic athletes, Tory Lanez One Umbrella, Weekends XO, or G-Unit all of whom he has worked within some capacity.

SYCB is part of the FabriKite group of companies which is a conglomerate of music, apparel, and tech.

Committed to social empowerment through social enterprise, Golam is a mentor for youth at the University of Toronto, Fashion Exchange, and Futurpreneur Toronto, Canada. Golam serves on the advisory board for the first free college for women in Shariatpur, Bangladesh, G H Khan College for Underprivileged Women (GHKMC) which provides free post-secondary education to 700 women annually. G.A. Khan is globally connected, multilingual, and speaks French, English and Bengali.
